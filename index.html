elizabeth witengier

Originally from Orlando, Elizabeth graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor’s in Advertising and a Minor in International Marketing. After working in the Advertising field for several years, she decided to pursue her love for children and advocacy—while incorporating creativity every step of the way. Elizabeth worked within various learning communities teaching reading and writing for almost 20 years. With the belief that creativity encourages and promotes learning, Elizabeth shared her passion for visual arts and creative writing with her students.

Her lessons and personal artwork incorporate her love for color and organic elements and are informed by her design training. Elizabeth is greatly inspired by the transformative nature of water and stories of perseverance. She strives to expand beyond the canvas and keyboard to evoke emotion with powerful branding strategies and digital marketing.

She currently works out of her studio in Orlando, FL, and is pursuing her Master’s in Web Design and Online Communications from the University of Florida. Go Gators!
